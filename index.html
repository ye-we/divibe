The Beat Goes On
  • Welcome to the rhythm machine! Your mission: become one with the beat
  • Watch for letters appearing on screen - they're your dance partners
  • Press both letters at the same time in sync with the music & targeted beat to score 50 points
  • Miss 4 beats in a row? That's a rhythm reset! (-10 points)
Your Control Center
  • Left Hand: A, S, D, F, Q, W, E, Z, X, C
  • Right Hand: J, K, L, ;, U, I, O, P, N, M
  • SPACE: Start/Pause the game
Power Moves
  • Tempo Control: Speed up or slow down (0.5x - 3x)
  • Target Beats: Choose which beats to play on (0-3). For example, "0,2" means play on the first and third beats of each bar
  • Visualizer: Watch the music come alive
  • Custom Tracks: Bring your own music to the party!
Tips From The Dev
  • Pro Tip: Stomp your feet or bob your head to the beat - trust me, it works!
  • Music Choice: Tracks with a steady tempo like house work best, but really, you can vibe to anything.
